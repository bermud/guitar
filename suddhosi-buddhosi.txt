Quechua

Kanmi yuraq, kanmi chekaq,

Kaypacha mosqoy mana tupayna.

Saqey chay watayta, saqey chay tutaychiytay,

Kausay chey chekaqpi jajay karu yupay chaipi.

Quechua Translation:
In white, in truth.
This reality dreams will not touch you. Leave attachments, leave the darkness. Live this life far away from illusion.
